About

Besides a sense of humor and levity, we have experience and understanding in the nuances between being successful and being…well….just ok.
The best business book ever written was the E-myth Revisited by Michael Gerber. I hope you read it. If not, that’s ok, here’s a quick recap…specialists start businesses. Specialists work their specialty. Specialists must transition to generalists, work on the business and not in the business, and hire specialists.
It’s that simple….and most new business owners will struggle for most of their business owning careers to understand it.
Fortunately for you…you found us, and we read the book. We lived the book. We help businesses and business owners overcome their challenges with compassion, understanding, empathetic reality checks, and straight talk to set you and your business on the right path.
We specialize in, arguably, the most critical areas of a business – Sales development, Marketing development, and Financial Management. We’ll help with HR too…if requested.
